CFD based design of flow & thermal systems


We are capable of design, analyse & test a system/components based on industrial requirements.



Using our expertise in the field, we can verify your designs based on numerical simulations. Further we will be able to suggest design improvements using parametric studies.


Identify & analyze the cause of component failures using numerical simulations. We can propose the rectification/modification by analyzing the flow field.

Consultancy services for R&D institutions

Based on industrial requirements, IFRL can design thermal and fluid system as per engineering standard.


We can collaborate with Academic institutions for industry/govt funded projects. IFRL have experience in such work from the proposal submission stage itself.

We have capabilities to design and fabricate Test bed/Experimental setups for the specific needs of industry/ academic institutions. Adequate training will be provided on conducting experiment and evaluation of results.
